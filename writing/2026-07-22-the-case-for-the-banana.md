Bananas are a magical yellow fruit. They taste like pineapple, cream, and mangoes combined. When you become a banana expert you realize it's not only a fruit but it's a berry and a herb. Not only is it a berry but it's the most important fruit to this planet. Hundreds of millions of people eat at least one banana. Without bananas the world would be ruined.

An interesting fact is that a banana is more of a berry than a strawberry. From a scientific view a berry is a fleshy fruit that develops from a single flower's ovary and contains seeds. This view of a berry perfectly matches a banana but not a strawberry. Bananas are a herb because it lacks the woody stem. That tall trunk looking thing on the banana is actually a pseudostem. In my opinion the best type of banana is number 8. Number 8 has the perfect ripeness.

![Bananas at every stage of ripeness, numbered 1 through 15](assets/images/banana-ripeness.jpg)

The bananas that are commercially grown are slowly going extinct. Think about what you would do every day without bananas. Then think about every other person that eats a banana daily. Without bananas people would not be getting enough calories and nutrition. Without enough calories people will lose weight and feel weak. Without the nutrition you would need to find a new way to get your potassium, vitamin B6 and fiber, every day. My idea is that every person or family grows their own banana tree. Not only will this save bananas from extinction but it will prevent future diseases from happening.

In conclusion, I suggest every person grows their individual banana tree. Not only will we save the banana species, but we will also fuel the bodies of everybody in the world. We must all work together to save the coolest and tastiest fruit of all, the banana.
