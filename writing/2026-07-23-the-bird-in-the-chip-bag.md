---
title: The Bird in the Chip Bag
date: 2026-07-23
tag: Personal Narrative
excerpt: What I saw out the car window one day, and why I still think about it.
---

One day, while riding in the car, my eyes stumbled upon a flying bird in the air. I didn't think anything of it at first, but when I looked closer I realized that the bird had a Lay's plastic bag of chips covering his head but also a whole flock of birds in the hundreds chasing it. I wondered to myself how a bird could possibly get a bag of Lay's chips and I realized it had probably taken the bag or found it somewhere littered.

This unexpected moment made me realize how human's litter and food can influence all of the birds' unexpected behavior. That one piece of litter gave me a memory I will remember forever, that one piece of litter changed all of the birds behavior.
